# Epistemic Matrix: Dialectical Tension Map

## Hickam_Orientation
This feature emerges from the recognition that terms across domains are rarely harmonious.
We reject the parsimonious urge to collapse polysemy into a single averaged definition.
Instead, we maintain the contradictions structurally.

- **[COMORBID: A]** Formal semantics demand parsimony.
- **[COMORBID: B]** Human cognition thrives on contradiction and polysemy.
- **[COMORBID: C]** AI systems naturally flatten polysemy into average consensus.

The Dialectical Tension Map holds these drivers in superposition.

### Symbolic Scars & Tension Markers
- **[⊘] Contradiction (Synthesis Gap):** Mutually exclusive requirements held in superposition.
- **[Φ] Golden Scar:** The unresolvable weighting between dominant and subordinate frames (1.618 / 1.000).
- **[∇] Uncertainty:** Aspects of intent or definition that remain underdetermined.

We do not resolve these tensions. We render them as aesthetic, intellectual montage.
